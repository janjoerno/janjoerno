DenCity TraQR

FOR A SAFE AND BETTER NEW NORMAL TRAVEL.

Web Application

DencityTraQR is a web application that monitors the population density of each place and establishment in Davao city with the use of the data from contact tracing providers and with digital maps. This is to ensure that customers can have the necessary information before going outside and for business owners to create plans to effectively control the visitors in their shops. Aside from that, it provides a convenient way to store multiple QR codes for various areas through the My QR feature
